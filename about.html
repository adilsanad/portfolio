About Me

Hi!

I’m Adil Sanad, an aspiring UX designer with a background in front-end development. I work on responsive web and mobile designs, and enjoy tackling challenges headfirst.

My first foray into the industry was with a new startup in Bangalore, India, called Ozibook where I started off as an intern. I gained a ton of invaluable experience and knowledge there and went on to lead the UX department.

I majored in Computer Science and Engineering at Dr. B R Ambedkar NIT Jalandhar, Punjab. My technical background allows me to create user experiences that bridge well between both design and dev teams, and allows for my designs to translate efficiently into developement.

When I’m not designing, I’m usually reading, playing video games, sketching, baking or enjoying some music.
